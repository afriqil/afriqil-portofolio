What I Did

End-to-end analytics workflow.

In this project, I analyzed Airbnb market data for New York City using an end-to-end data analyst workflow. I started by auditing and cleaning raw datasets, validating missing values, data types, duplicates, and key business fields such as price, availability, reviews, host attributes, location, and estimated revenue.

After preparing the data, I performed exploratory data analysis across boroughs, neighbourhoods, room types, pricing, availability, reviews, and estimated revenue. I also conducted statistical analysis to compare market performance across listing characteristics and prepared structured outputs for business interpretation.

For dashboard development, I created dashboard-ready fact and dimension tables, exported Power BI-ready CSV files, built DAX measures for KPIs, and designed a 4-page interactive Power BI dashboard to communicate market performance, pricing behavior, location trends, and host insights.

Key Insights

What the analysis revealed.

Location concentration

Manhattan and Brooklyn dominate Airbnb listing supply across New York City.

Revenue leadership

Manhattan generates the highest estimated revenue among NYC boroughs.

Room type mix

Entire homes/apartments represent the largest share of Airbnb listings.

Pricing variation

Pricing and revenue vary significantly by room type, capacity, location, and host profile.

Host profile effect

Superhost and host portfolio segments show different revenue and performance patterns.

Business Recommendations

Recommended actions from the analysis.

Segment pricing strategy

Apply pricing strategies by borough and room type instead of using a single market-wide pricing approach.

Separate supply and revenue markets

Monitor high-revenue neighbourhoods separately from high-supply neighbourhoods because the two do not always overlap.

Use capacity segmentation

Evaluate capacity and room type together to better understand revenue optimization opportunities.

Compare host performance

Use superhost and host portfolio segmentation to understand differences in revenue, listing volume, and guest experience.

Enable area-level decisions

Use dashboard filters to support borough, neighbourhood, room type, and host-segment analysis.
